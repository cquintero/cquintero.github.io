Embarking on a Learning Sabbatical?

Self-led learning can be lonely! High quality educational materials are easy to find, but mentors and peers are not.

‍

The best learning occurs in communities of practice, not in isolation. Peers help with accountability, motivation, and social isolation. Mentors suggest resources and critique deliverables.

‍

If you want help planning a learning sabbatical, or finding community during one, book a 30min call with Chris who will try to connect you with others focused on similar topics.
