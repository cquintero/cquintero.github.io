Learning Sabbatical Write ups

‍

‍

Learning sabbaticals are time away from work focused on acquiring knowledge or skills. Most last between three months and two years.

‍

They are typically done full-time or with a minimal amount of part-time work. Some focus on research, projects, and short courses. Others include stints of contract or volunteer work. Most importantly, they are self led.
